About Me

Hi there! I'm Amy, and if you didn't know that then you're definitely in the right place. I graduated from the University of Minnesota with a degrees in Speech-Language-Hearing Sciences and Neuroscience. Currently I'm working as an Operations and Production Manager at a nonprofit in Northeast Minneapolis that provides employment services to individuals with barriers to employment. Although this wasn't the path I imagined for myself upon graduation, it's been an incredible learning experience that I wouldn't trade for anything.

Outside of work, I have about a million different interests that get pushed aside far too often. When spare time does finally arise, I enjoy traveling, cooking, eating, photography (the background image is one I took!), ultimate frisbee, disc golf, video games, reading, playing dungeons and dragons with my closest friends, and indulging in my guiltiest pleasure: watching The Bachelor. I long for evenings spent with friends huddled over the game table, discussing strategy, and the chilly fall nights spent at The Cedar Inn after our weekly ultimate frisbee league games. I look forward to when life allows such gatherings again, but until then I'll just always be coding.
